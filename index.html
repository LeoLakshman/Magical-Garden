My Magical Garden

Explore the fascinating world of magical flowers as an enthusiastic plant-explorer in the world of Codédex! Each seed has an unique and mysterious trait whose secrets will be exposed by breeding the plants with each other. Try it out yourself and reveal the magic and histories behind these flowers!

Game Rules: Combine the seeds, seedlings and flowers together by picking them up with your cursor, dragging them to the same species (seed, seedling, flower) and eventually dropping them. The notebook on your left helps you to memorize all formulas of the breeding plants. The flowerbed on your right shows you which flowers you have already discovered. Fill out the whole flowerbed to successfully complete your notebook and finish your journey as a plant-explorer in this magical garden of Codédex!

Don't forget to turn on the wonderful music of this magical garden by clicking on the soundbutton in your top-left corner! Click on the button to start the game and begin your journey!
